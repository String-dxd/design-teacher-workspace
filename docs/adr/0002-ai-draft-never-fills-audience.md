# AI Draft fills content and settings, never the audience

AI Draft populates a post's **content** (title, description, response type, due
date, and an optional follow-up question) and **settings defaults** (a reminder
before the due date). It deliberately never selects **recipients**,
staff-in-charge, enquiry email, or attachments.

## Why

Choosing who receives a post — above all a consent request — is a deliberate,
per-student, sensitive act. An AI guessing the audience is the one autofill whose
error causes real-world harm: a consent request delivered to the wrong families.
The teacher always chooses the audience by hand. Content and a reminder are safe
to prefill because they are fully visible and editable before the teacher picks
recipients and posts.

## Consequences

- The draft is never "ready to send" straight from AI Draft — recipient selection
  is always a required, human step, which is the intended safety property.
